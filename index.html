What this dashboard is saying
VHCB’s strongest value comes from solving a dual-mission problem: deliver affordable homes while protecting the land systems that make Vermont resilient.
Executive headline
1.7×
VHCB capital is leveraging significant partner investment while keeping a meaningful share of units permanently affordable.
Recruiter-ready insights
Housing pressure is concentrated, not uniform. Chittenden, Franklin, and Lamoille emerge as the most urgent pressure zones.
Conservation risk and housing need overlap. Orange and Franklin reflect the kind of dual-mission tension VHCB is uniquely positioned to manage.
Leverage and project mix matter. New construction and redevelopment projects are driving both scale and long-term affordability outcomes.
Program story is visible in the data. This isn’t just project tracking — it’s a portfolio view of mission delivery.
